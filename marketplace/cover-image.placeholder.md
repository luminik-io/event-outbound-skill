# Cover image — design brief

**File to produce:** `cover-image.png`
**Dimensions:** 1600 × 900 px (16:9). Marketplace also displays a 1:1 crop centred on the frame; keep the focal content inside a 900 × 900 safe zone in the middle.
**Format:** PNG, under 400 KB. No transparent background — the marketplace grid uses dark theme.
**File weight budget:** target under 250 KB so the marketplace index stays fast.

## Visual direction

Not a logo. Not a hero product shot. Think "editorial cover" for a B2B tool — Hey.com / Pitch-style.

**Primary frame:** a single email draft, rendered in a flat mock-up (no real inbox chrome). Subject line visible in lowercase: `saastr annual plan`. Body visible below it, two lines clipped. Faint callouts on the right margin showing which rules the draft passed:

```
subject ≤ 4 words         ✓
body 50–100 words         ✓
you/we ratio 3.2          ✓
no banned phrases          ✓
```

Keep the tick marks understated — a thin sans-serif, light grey.

**Secondary frame (smaller, behind):** a timeline strip showing the six-touch cadence (LI connect at -28d, email at -14d, LI nudge at -7d, LI day-of, email +2d, LI +7d). Very light lines, just enough to imply structure.

## Typography

- Subject line and body: Inter or IBM Plex Sans. Regular weight, not bold.
- Rule labels: same font, 80% opacity.

## Palette

- Background: #0B0D10 (very dark near-black; matches marketplace dark theme).
- Email surface: #F5F5F3 (off-white paper).
- Accent: #E6E150 (Luminik yellow) for a single thin underline under the subject line. Nothing else in accent.
- Secondary text: #8A8F98.

## What to avoid

- No AI-generated stock art. No human faces. No calendar icons. No "AI" badges.
- No emoji, no sparkles, no gradients.
- No words like "AI-powered", "revolutionary", "transform".
- No stock conference photography.

## Reference moodboard (for the freelancer)

- The Bezos shareholder-letter 2025 cover.
- Pitch.com "for work" illustrations.
- Figma Config 2024 session card backgrounds.

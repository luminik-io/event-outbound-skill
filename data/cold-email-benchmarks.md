# Cold Email Benchmarks from Gong / 30MPC / Outbound Squad Report

This document synthesizes key findings from the Gong / 30MPC / Outbound Squad data report on cold email performance. These benchmarks guide the event-outbound skill's email generation and validation processes.

## Subject Line Rules
- **Max Word Count:** 4 words
- **Case:** All lowercase
- **Focus:** Priority-based (speak to the reader's priorities, not the seller's pitch)
- **Empty Subjects:** Banned
- **Buzzwords/Numbers/Social Proof:** Banned. Avoid words like: AI, platform, leverage, synergy, solution, best-in-class, revolutionary, paradigm, game-changer, transform, unlock, supercharge, data, growth, scale, innovate, optimize, efficiency, ROI, metrics, strategy, ecosystem, empower, drive, cutting-edge, next-gen, future-proof, disrupt, seamless, dynamic, holistic, integrated, proactive, reactive, robust, scalable, streamlined, turnkey, value-added, vertical, horizontal, omnichannel, frictionless, low-hanging fruit, move the needle, deep dive, bandwidth, brainstorm, circle back, synergize, win-win, thought leadership, bleeding edge.

## Email Body Length Targets
- **Word Count:** 50-100 words
- **Sentence Count:** 3-4 sentences
- **Structure:** Personalization → Problem → Solution → CTA

## Pronoun Ratio
- **Bias:** Majority "you/your" over "we/our"

## Call to Action (CTA) Type Ranking (with Reply Rate Deltas)
- **Make Offer:** +28% reply rate
- **Ask for Interest:** +7% reply rate
- **Ask for Problem:** -29% reply rate
- **Ask for Meeting:** -44% reply rate

## Banned Words and Phrases (Hard Filter)
- Sales-cliche fillers: "happy to", "excited to", "hope this finds you well", "hope this", "hope you", "just checking in", "circling back", "touching base", "wanted to reach out", "quick question", "at your convenience", "looking forward to hearing"
- Buzzword verbs/nouns: "revolutionary", "leverage", "synergy", "solution", "best-in-class", "paradigm", "game-changer", "transform", "unlock", "supercharge"
- Gating phrases (permission-based doctrine: attach or link the asset, never gate it): "want me to send", "want me to drop", "want me to meet", "yours to keep", "if it's of interest"
- Defensive throat-clearing: "not a pitch", "no pitch", "peer channel", "no strings", "no pressure"

## Exclamation Marks & Emoji
- **Exclamation Marks:** Zero
- **Emoji:** Zero

## Personalization Tier Ranking
1. Activity-based
2. Company
3. Individual
4. Industry
5. Baseline
